    Database and/with Collaboration

    • Rice Population Reference Panel (RPRP)

      • 2023-01, We released the first version of RPRP (v1.0).

      Here we present our pan-genome resouses and the research progress of RPRP pan-genome study. We also share with the community with our project description, pan-genome dataset (i.e., genome assemblies, annotations, TEs, standing natural structure varation (SV) and large SVs), and softwares, tools with scripts there."

      Publicaion:

      1. Zhou Y (#1), Yu Z (#1), Chebotarov D (#1), Chougule, K(#1),. et al. Nature Communications, 2023, 14(1): 1567.

      2. Zhou Y (#1), Chebotarov D (#1), Kudrna D, et al. Scientific data, 2020, 7(1): 113.

      Publicaion:

    • Gramene

      • 2023-05, We released large SNP datasets of rice, sorghum, maize, and soybean, inlcuding the most recently release high-quality genomes, e.g., Rice magic16,Tx2783, Tx436, and TX430, B73v5, Mo17v2, JD17, for downloading

      This dataset could also be downloaded with DOI registration form KAUST repository DOI: 10.25781/KAUST-12WKO
