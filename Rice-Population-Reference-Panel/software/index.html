Below are the list of tools in the 4 workflows.

workflow 1: call SVs (INVs and PAVs), by NGMLR + SVIM, i.e. INV and DEL (PAV)

workflow 2: call SVs (INVs and PAVs), by NGMLR + sniffles, i.e. INV and DEL (PAV)

workflow 3: call SVs (INVs and PAVs), including INV, by minimap2 and syri

workflow 4: call SVs (INVs and PAVs), by numcer and syri
